VIJAY ANTONY SONGS

Pichaikkaaran (transl. Beggar) is a 2016 Indian Tamil-language action horror film written and directed by Sasi, produced by Fatima Vijay Antony and starring Vijay Antony and Satna Titus, with Bagavathi Perumal, Vazhakku En Muthuraman, and Dheepa Ramanujam in supporting roles. Antony also composed the music for the film.[2][3] The story is about Arul (Vijay Antony), a billionaire businessman who leads a 48-day long secret life as a beggar as part of a religious offering to save his hopeless dying mother. Pichaikkaaran was released on 4 March 2016 and positive reviews from critics where it became an blockbuster at the box office.[4] The film was remade in Odia as Baby, in Marathi as Bhikari and in Kannada as Amma I Love You
