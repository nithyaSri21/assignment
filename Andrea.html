ANDREA SONGS

Andrea Maria Jeremiah is an Indian playback singer and actress who works predominantly in Tamil films apart from a few Telugu and Malayalam language films.[1] She made her playback singing debut with "Kannum Kannum Nokia" in the Tamil film Anniyan, with music composed by Harris Jayaraj. She made her acting debut in the Tamil film Pachaikili Muthucharam (2007), with R. Sarathkumar and Jyothika and Malayalam debut with Annayum Rasoolum (2013) with Fahadh Faasil.
