ALIA BHATT MOVIES





Alia Bhatt (/ˈɑːliə ˈbʌt/; born 15 March 1993) is a British actress of Indian descent who predominantly works in Hindi films.[1][2] Known for her portrayals of women in troubling circumstances, she has received several accolades, including a National Film Award and six Filmfare Awards. One of India's highest-paid actresses, Time magazine honoured her with the Time100 Impact Award in 2022 and named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2024.
