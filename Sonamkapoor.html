SONAM KAPOOR MOVIES





Sonam Kapoor Ahuja (pronounced [soːnəm kəˈpuːr]; born 9 June 1985) is an Indian actress who works in Hindi films. She has received several awards, including a National Film Award and a Filmfare Award. One of the highest-paid actresses in India as of 2018,[1] Kapoor appeared in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list from 2012 to 2016.
